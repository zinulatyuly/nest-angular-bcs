Who you are

After graduating from Houghton College I started my first job teaching 2nd grade at Maple West Elementary School in Williamsville, NY. Williamsville was a great school district in which to work. I collaborated with highly qualified teachers, attended outstanding professional development and worked with wonderful students. I remember each group of students with fondness. There was a diversity of ability levels, ethnic and cultural backgrounds and personalities. While teaching I attended Buffalo State College to earn my Master’s of Education Degree.
